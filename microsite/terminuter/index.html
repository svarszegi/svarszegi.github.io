TERMINUTER frees up your time

The writing of meeting minutes is a cumbersome task. Furthermore, meeting participants who take detailed notes typically find it difficult to actively contribute at the same time. TERMINUTER addresses these issues by writing and structuring your meeting minutes for you. The solution interacts with participants during and after meetings and can produce minutes in template form, thus allowing you to get the most out of your meetings. For example, if a task is assigned during a meeting without a deadline or owner, TERMINUTER asks in order to clarify. In this way, clearly defined action items are obtained.

From a minimum viable product (MVP) to an enhanced version

TERMINUTER offers tremendous benefits in both the short and long term as, unlike comparable products, it offers far more than simple transcription. For example, a search function for past meeting minutes will allow any company to tap the immense pool of knowledge that is being built up through meeting discussions.
