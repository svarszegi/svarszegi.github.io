Professional skills 

A digital creative

I am engaged in multiple fields of digital design, from video animation to front-end development with a rich visual culture thanks to my background in photography.

I am never resting and am constantly trying to evolve and learn. I found my new passion in UX design and front-end development, boosted by expertise gained in IBM Design Thinking and Agile.

Having worked with small to large businesses as a contractor as well as a member and leader of cross competency teams in a multi-national environment I know how to live up the customer expectations and meet their needs with excellent delivery.

Sharing with colleagues has top priority for me. The best way to learn and improve is a direct chat about an issue.

Tools used for design: Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Indesign, Experience Design, Muse, Axure, InVision, Mobirise. 

Tools used for video editing and animation: Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere and Adobe Audition.

Tools for development: HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Javascript, JQuery, Angular, Node, Git, Gulp.
