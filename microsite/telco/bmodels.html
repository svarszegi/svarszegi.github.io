New operating models with dramatically lower operating costs

CSP business models have changed drastically, shifting from a relatively simple model to a complex and competitive one in which the volume of data sold rises yet revenues fall. Now, CSPs must satisfy their more demanding customers while increasing revenues and driving costs down. However, we believe there is no contradiction in a new model that combines increased effectiveness with dramatically reduced cost.
